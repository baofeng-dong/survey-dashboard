Introduction

Overview

Starting October 26th, 2016, TriMet staff surveying riders on board buses and trains. We’re asking a series of questions about trip origin/destination, trip purpose, frequency of transit use, and your transit experience, as part of an ongoing research study that helps inform our programs and policies.

What’s in the survey?

We’ll be asking a series of questions such as:

  • Where are you starting and ending your trip?
  • How often are you riding TriMet compared to a year ago?
  • How important is the price of gas in your decision to ride or not ride transit?
  • How satisfied are you with the trips you make on TriMet?
  • How would you get to places if TriMet was not an option?

To learn more about this survey, you can contact us at gissurvey@trimet.org or call 503-238-RIDE (7433).
